About

Rounak Saha Niloy is the Principal Investigator and founder of The DRAFT Lab, where he leads cutting-edge research in naval architecture and marine engineering. With over 8 years of experience in computational fluid dynamics and machine learning applications, Dr. Jahin has established himself as a pioneer in integrating artificial intelligence with traditional naval architecture practices.

His research philosophy centers on bridging the gap between theoretical naval architecture and practical marine engineering solutions. Dr. Jahin's work has significantly contributed to the development of AI-driven ship design methodologies, revolutionizing how we approach vessel optimization and hydrodynamic performance prediction.

Under his leadership, The DRAFT Lab has become a hub for innovative research, fostering collaboration between academia and industry to address real-world challenges in marine transportation and offshore engineering.
