Design, Research and Analysis in Fluid Technologies Lab

(DRAFT Lab)

Cutting-edge research in machine learning, computational fluid dynamics, and naval architecture

Explore Our Research

Research Areas

Machine Learning

Developing advanced machine learning algorithms for maritime applications including ship performance optimization, predictive maintenance, and autonomous navigation systems for marine vessels.

Computational Fluid Dynamics

Advanced CFD simulations for marine hydrodynamics, wave-structure interactions, propeller design optimization, and hull form analysis to improve vessel efficiency and performance.

Naval Architecture

Innovative ship design methodologies, structural analysis of marine vessels, stability studies, and seakeeping performance analysis for next-generation maritime transportation systems.
